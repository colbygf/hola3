  • Our Story

    Dream Motion was founded in 2023 by a pair of former long-time local government employees. After years of working through red-tape, developing our own procedures, integrating data, and performing analysis we realized just how difficult it is to develop and scale data solutions at the local-government level. We were particularly alarmed by the lack of expertise and dedication of many contractors tasked with aiding municipalities in developing data solutions. In addition to this, it became evident that a lack of staffing resources was going to be a long-term problem for data teams at the local government level. That's where we decided we could make a real impact. Dream Motion is here to bridge the gap between large corporations eying to make a quick buck off of local municipalities and the often impossible internal development. We are here to aid local governments, NPOs, and other organizations in developing internal procedures, frameworks, and systems harnessing scarce resources to drive real change in both operational efficiency and decision-making.

  • Solution Development

    Specializing in integrating data sources and cloud migration, Dream Motion ensures seamless data flow and accessibility, enabling organizations to make informed decisions and drive positive change in the most efficient way possible.

  • Assessment Services

    Dream Motion provides comprehensive assessment services to evaluate existing data structures and processes, identifying areas for improvement and optimization.

  • Monitoring

    Through procedural changes and infrastructure development, we enable you to monitor the success of your agencies and programs.,

  • Driving Change

    At Dream Motion we know first hand that monetary resources for governmental programs are finite and need to be stretched as far as possible. To help you properly serve the community, we will develop procedures and tools aimed at predicting costs, risks, and overall impact to weigh the best interventions before you implement them.

  • Resource Efficiency

    The end goal of Dream Motion's work is to drive resource and operational efficiency for your organization. Whether your organization uses data consistently or is just starting to collect data, we will work together to streamline workflows and drive efficient resource use. Often times in local government staffing resources for data are scarce, this leads to high workloads for data staff and delegation to other staff. Our job is to drive those workloads down by refining processes and establishing infrastructure.
